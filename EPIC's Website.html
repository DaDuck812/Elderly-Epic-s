ELDERLY EPIC'S

What is EPIC's?

EPIC's is a project meant to benefit the community. Projects are chosen for EPIC's based off of a couple of things
1.) It benefits an issue a community is facing.
2.) It is feasible to accomplish
3.) The peers you work with all accept the project
4.) It fits within the given budget
5.) You find a stakeholder willing to work with you

What do we do?

Our project is an app that helps the elderly,
whether that's with healthcare communication,
communication between them and their caregiver or doctor,
or just an app that can help track their health and allow
them to talk to friends and family.
